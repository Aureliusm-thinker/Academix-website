Welcome to Academix

Our History

Academix was founded in 2020 by a group of passionate educators and technologists with a vision to democratize education globally. Recognizing the potential of the internet to connect learners with knowledge resources, they set out to create a platform that would revolutionize how education is accessed and delivered. The founders, who had backgrounds in academia and technology, aimed to address two key challenges: accessibility and quality. They envisioned a platform where anyone, anywhere in the world, could access high-quality educational content from leading instructors and institutions. This vision laid the foundation for Academix.
