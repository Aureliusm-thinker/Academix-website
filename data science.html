Introduction to Data Science

Data science is an interdisciplinary field that uses scientific methods, algorithms, and systems to extract knowledge and insights from structured and unstructured data. It encompasses a wide range of techniques from statistics, machine learning, data mining, and big data analytics to process and analyze large datasets. The goal is to uncover patterns, derive actionable insights, and make data-driven decisions.
